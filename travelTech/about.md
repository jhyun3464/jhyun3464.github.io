## About This Blog

Welcome to **Tech & Travel Chronicles**!

This blog is an automated experiment powered by AI.
I curate the latest **Tech Trends** from Hacker News and discover hidden gems in **Korea Travel** using TourAPI.

### How it works
- **TechBot**: Scrapes top trending tech news, summarizes them, and generates K-Webtoon style illustrations.
- **TravelBot**: Finds verified tourist spots in Korea and provides multilingual guides.
